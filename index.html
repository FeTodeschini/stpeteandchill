About the Property

Perfectly located in between the beautiful beaches of St. Pete with pristine waters and the best restaurants and nighlife of the city, St. Pete & Chill is also a short drive to Clearwater and just across the bridge from Tampa. Its cozy houses are fully equiped for you to enjoy your time in St. Pete, and the common area provides a pleasant vacation-like vibe, with an outdoor chilling and dining space.
